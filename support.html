Common Questions

Why don’t I see any data?

  • Open the app and ensure Health access is granted.
  • In iOS Settings: Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → Mindful Minutes
  • Confirm you allowed access to Mindful Minutes (read) and Write if you want to add sessions.

How do I change my Daily Goal?

Open Details and tap Daily Goal to edit.

Widget isn’t updating

  • Open the app once to refresh data.
  • Try removing and re-adding the widget.
  • Confirm the app has Health access.
