Privacy Policy

Mindful Minutes

Short version: Mindful Minutes does not collect, store, or share personal data. Your mindful minutes data stays on your device in Apple Health.

Data Collection

Mindful Minutes does not collect personal information, does not create user accounts, and does not transmit your data to any servers.

Apple Health (HealthKit)

Mindful Minutes can read and write mindful minutes using Apple Health (HealthKit), only if you grant permission.

  • Read: Used to display your mindful minutes by day and month.
  • Write: Used when you manually add a mindful session from within the app.

Mindful Minutes does not store your Health data outside of Apple Health and does not send Health data off your device.

Analytics and Tracking

Mindful Minutes does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.
